About the Game

Rubik's Cube 3D brings the timeless puzzle to your browser with a modern twist. Built with Three.js, this game offers a fully interactive 3D experience. Whether you're a beginner or a speedcubing pro, challenge yourself to solve the cube or let the auto-solver show you the way.

Features like move tracking, reset options, and a help guide make it easy to dive in. Play now and see how fast you can solve the cube!
