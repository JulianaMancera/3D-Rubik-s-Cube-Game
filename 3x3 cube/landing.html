About the Game

Rubik's Cube 3D brings the classic puzzle to life in your browser. Powered by Three.js, it delivers a fully interactive 3D experience with smooth controls and vibrant visuals.

Whether you're a beginner or a speedcubing pro, enjoy features like move tracking, reset options, and an auto-solver. Dive in and test your skills today!
